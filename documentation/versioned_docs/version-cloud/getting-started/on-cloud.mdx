# Sign Up

This guide walks you through signing up for WSO2 Agent Manager on the cloud.

## Step 1: Sign In

Go to [console.agent-manager.cloud.wso2.com](https://console.agent-manager.cloud.wso2.com/) and sign in with your **Google** or **GitHub** account.

## Step 2: Create Your Organization

You'll be asked to create an organization. This is your workspace where all your projects and agents live.

- **Organization Name** - a display name for your org (e.g., `Acme Corp`)
- **Organization Handle** - a short identifier used in workspace URLs (e.g., `acme`). You can't change this later, so pick something that fits your brand.

Click **Create Organization** to continue.

## Step 3: Access the Console

Once your organization is created, you'll land on the Agent Manager console and receive a welcome email at your registered address.

From here, you're ready to [add your first agent](./create-your-first-agent.mdx).

By signing up and using WSO2 Agent Manager, you agree to the [Terms of Use](https://wso2.com/cloud/agent-manager/terms-of-use).
